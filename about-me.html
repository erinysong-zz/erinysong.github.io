about me

Hi! I'm Erin and welcome to my personal website! I'm a sophomore at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science and Data Science with a focus on design. My passions lie in the intersection of technology, human behavior, and social impact.

Currently, I'm exploring my interests in software development and enterpreneurship as a Software Engineering Intern at Waterline Data. I also serve as Professional Development Month Chair for Berkeley's Society of Women Engineers, where I partner with local tech companies to organize professional recruiting workshops.
