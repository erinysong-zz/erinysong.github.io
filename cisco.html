Cisco Systems

Software Engineering Intern

Summer 2019 I worked with Cisco's Data and Analaytics team to develop software for Cisco Ready, an internal customer sales dashboard primarily used by the sales and Customer Experience departments.

Because Cisco was transitioning from a one-time payment to a subscription-based consumer model, the Customer Experience team relied heavily on data on their customers' subscription renewals. With this in mind, I focused on developing software that visualized customer data in the most accessible and useful way possible, while also staying consistent with Cisco's design guidelines.

Technologies

For the backend, I used tools such as SQL, SAP HANA, Java, and Tableau, to consolidate, analyze, and visualize different datasets on Cisco's internal sales data that provided the most useful insights on product success.

For the frontend, I used HTML, CSS, and Angular.js to develop UI code displaying product subscription data in the most accessible way possible for the sales team.
