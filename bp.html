BLUEPRINT, TECH FOR NONPROFITS

SUMMARY

Cal Blueprint is a campus organization at UC Berkeley whose mission is to make technology accessible and useful for social good. Every semester, the organization develops probono software for nonprofits and promotes the intersection between tech and social good through various events.

I currently serve as Blueprint's Director of Outreach, with my primary goals including the following:

1) Strengthen Blueprint's external presence by initiating new chapters on other college campuses (currently chapters in Berkeley and Waterloo).

2) Register Blueprint as international nonprofit organization aimed to develop technology for organizations achieving social good.

3) Partner with other organizations to promote development for social good and further expand conversation on technology and human impact.

If you are interested in working with Blueprint in any of the means mentioned above or in a way that you think can promote tech for social good, please feel free to contact me for more information!
